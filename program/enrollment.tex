Students are expected to successfully complete any precursor courses in the program.
If they fail to complete a precursor course, they may be removed from the subsequent
courses and required to retake the incomplete course on the next available date before
continuing with the program.
\par
\bigskip
Students may be removed from a course for failing to attend for two consecutive weeks,
or for having any past due tuition and fees due to the College.
\par
\bigskip
Students who are not enrolled in any courses at the college for five days may be removed from their program.
