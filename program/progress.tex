The expectation is that students will attend 100\% of the time.
However, students must maintain the minimum overall program attendance
of 80\% and progress of 80\%.
\par
\bigskip
Course specific attendance and progress requirements are the same.
Students must complete all required course objectives and modules
before completing the program.
\par
\bigskip
If a student falls below the above minimum attendance or progress
percentage, they may be subject to disciplinary action.
Absences are not excused. Students are expected to communicate
with their instructors prior to any known absences or within 24 hours.
It is the student’s responsibility to determine what course work was
missed and what is required of them.
\par
\bigskip
Students are expected to be on time, ready to learn/work when
the class begins. Students who are tardy or who are not actively
engaged during class/lab time may be marked nonproductive.
