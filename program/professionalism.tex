Part of Dixie Technical Technical College's mission is to prepare its students
to work in a professional environment. Students are expected to conduct
themselves in a business-like and professional manner on campus and treat
others with dignity and respect.
\par
\bigskip
Students should dress appropriately for the occupational environment for
which they are training and start acquiring apparel suitable for employment.
Clothing should be appropriate for safety and effective performance of tasks
in the area of training in which they are enrolled. College students and
employees are expected to be clean, neatly groomed, and free of body odor.
They should be considerate of others who may be sensitive to strong fragrance.
Students should look professional in appearance at all times and should be free
from distracting attire/jewelry.
\par
\bigskip
Offensive, abusive, insulting, vulgar, obscene, degrading, morbid, lascivious
or erotic language which interferes with the education of other students is prohibited.
Students must refrain from exhibiting offensive personal habits on campus which are
distracting to other students or College staff.
