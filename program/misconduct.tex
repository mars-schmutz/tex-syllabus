Academic misconduct is prohibited and includes,
but is not limited to, cheating, plagiarism, forgery,
misrepresentation of identity or attendance, accessing
answers to assignments, unauthorized use of a phone or
other technology during an exam or lab, taking tests
with the intention of a retake, providing or receiving
the content of an exam or test prior to its administration,
or presenting others’ work as your own or knowingly allowing
another student to submit your work as theirs.
\par
\bigskip
Knowingly or recklessly furnishing false information to College
officials, faculty and/or staff also constitutes academic misconduct.
This includes forgery or alteration of College documents, records, or
identification.
